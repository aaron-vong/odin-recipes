Pizza Recipe

Description

A classic homemade pizza with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and melted cheese—perfect for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add pepperoni slices if using, and sprinkle oregano over everything.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve.
