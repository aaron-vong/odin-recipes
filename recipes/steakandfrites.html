Steak and Frites Recipe

Description

A quick and flavorful Korean-inspired ground beef dish with a savory-sweet sauce, perfect over rice or noodles for an easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 Ribeye or Sirloin steaks (about 8 oz each)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 large russet potatoes, cut into fries
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil (for fries)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) for the fries.
  2. Toss the potato fries with vegetable oil, salt, and pepper, then spread on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  4. Season the steaks with salt and pepper on both sides.
  5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Cook the steaks 3-5 minutes per side for medium-rare (adjust for thickness).
  7. Add butter, smashed garlic, and herbs to the pan and baste the steaks for extra flavor.
  8. Remove steaks from the pan and let them rest for 5 minutes.
  9. Serve steaks with the crispy fries on the side.
